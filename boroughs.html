Boroughs Film Fest is an annual event that focuses on three major cities.

The movies all have one thing in common; the city is a major character. Though the term 'borough' describes a subdivision, Boroughs Film Fest emphasizes how a location can unite thousands of people.

A website, merchandise, poster, and logo were designed for the event.
