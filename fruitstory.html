iOS Application

Portfolio

This app prototype was created out of the challenge to change a consumer behavior, with Edible Arrangements being the client. The behavior: to increase fruit consumption. It focuses on children to start habits early, and uses your phones flash projector to show a virtual display right on the fruit itself. It's a personal interactive exhibit-to-go, delighting children and getting them excited about making healthy choices.

There is also a Nutrition Facts mode, organized by three search options: typing, browsing a fruit list, or scanning a fruit sticker. You'll see an infographic with nutrition and vitamin information. It even lets you know when the best season for that fruit is!
